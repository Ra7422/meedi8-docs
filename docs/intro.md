# Welcome to Meedi8 Help Center

Meedi8 is an **AI-powered mediation platform** that helps people resolve conflicts using Nonviolent Communication (NVC) principles.

## What is Meedi8?

Meedi8 provides a safe, structured space for conflict resolution. Our AI mediator, trained in NVC techniques, guides conversations toward understanding and resolution.

**Key features:**
- **Solo Sessions** - Work through your thoughts privately with Meedi
- **Duo Mediation** - Structured conversations with another person, guided by AI
- **Professional Reports** - Get insights and action items after each session
- **Telegram Import** - Provide context by importing chat history

## How It Works

### 1. Share Your Perspective
Start by telling Meedi about the situation. You can type, record voice messages, or upload files for context.

### 2. Get Coached
Meedi helps you identify your feelings and needs using NVC principles, preparing you for productive dialogue.

### 3. Invite the Other Person (Optional)
For duo mediation, share a link with the other person. They'll go through their own coaching session.

### 4. Mediated Conversation
In duo sessions, Meedi facilitates a turn-by-turn conversation, keeping things constructive and focused on understanding.

### 5. Resolution & Report
Receive a professional report with key insights, agreed actions, and next steps.

Meedi8 uses the latest AI models from leading providers, each chosen for specific strengths:

### Anthropic (Claude)

| Model | What It Does |
|-------|--------------|
| **Claude Sonnet 4** | Powers all real-time conversations - solo coaching, pre-mediation preparation, and duo mediation sessions. Chosen for its empathetic communication style and deep understanding of NVC principles. |
| **Claude Sonnet 4.5** | Generates professional reports and session summaries. Selected for its analytical capabilities and ability to produce therapist-quality insights. |
| **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** | Analyzes uploaded images and screenshots for context. Used when you share visual content during sessions. |

### OpenAI

| Model | What It Does |
|-------|--------------|
| **GPT-4** | Alternative report generation for comprehensive mediation assessments. Provides a different analytical perspective for clinical reports. |

### Why Multiple Models?

We use different AI models because each excels at different tasks:

- **Real-time coaching** needs empathy, patience, and conversational skill - Claude Sonnet 4 is exceptional at this
- **Report generation** requires analytical depth and structured output - Claude Sonnet 4.5 delivers professional-grade summaries
- **Image analysis** needs vision capabilities - Claude 3.5 Sonnet handles visual context
- **Diverse perspectives** in reports can surface different insights - GPT-4 complements Claude's analysis

All models are trained to follow Nonviolent Communication (NVC) principles and maintain a supportive, non-judgmental approach throughout your mediation journey.
