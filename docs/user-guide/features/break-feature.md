# Taking Breaks

Need a moment? The break feature lets you pause without losing progress.

## Why Take Breaks?

Breaks are important for:
- Processing emotions
- Calming down if heated
- Taking time to think
- Physical needs

---

## How to Request a Break

### In Solo Sessions
Simply close the tab or say "I need a break." Your progress is auto-saved.

### In Duo Mediation

1. Click the **"Break"** button
2. Choose break duration (5, 10, 15 min or custom)
3. Both parties are notified
4. Session pauses

---

## During a Break

### What Happens
- Timer counts down
- Both can see remaining time
- Meedi sends return reminder
- Session stays secure

### What You Can Do
- Step away
- Get water
- Breathe deeply
- Come back refreshed

---

## Returning from Break

### When Timer Ends
- You'll get a notification
- Click to return
- Resume where you left off

### Early Return
Both parties can return early if ready.

---

## Tips

:::tip Take Breaks Early
Don't wait until you're overwhelmed.
:::

:::tip Communicate
Let the other person know why you need a break.
:::

:::tip Use the Time
Actually rest - don't stew on the conflict.
:::

:::tip No Shame
Breaks are healthy and encouraged.
:::

---

## Automatic Pauses

Meedi may suggest breaks when:
- Conversation gets heated
- Strong emotions are detected
- Someone seems overwhelmed

---

## Session Timeout

If inactive for extended periods:
- Session auto-saves
- You can return anytime
- Just log back in
