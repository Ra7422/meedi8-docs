# Achievements

Earn badges and XP as you grow with Meedi8. Achievements celebrate your progress and encourage continued engagement.

---

## How to View Achievements

1. Open the **Navigation Panel** (tap the menu button)
2. Select **Personal Growth**
3. Tap **Achievements**

You'll see your progress summary and all available badges.

---

## Understanding Badges

### Rarity Levels

| Rarity | Color | Difficulty |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Common** | Green | Achievable early in your journey |
| **Rare** | Blue | Requires consistent effort |
| **Epic** | Purple | Significant milestone |
| **Legendary** | Gold | Elite achievement |

### Visibility Types

- **Visible** - You can see the badge and requirements
- **Silhouette** - You see a hint but not full details (aspirational)
- **Secret** - Hidden until you unlock them (surprises!)

## Tips for Earning Achievements

:::tip Start with Common Badges
Focus on the green (common) badges first. They're designed to get you started and build momentum.
:::

:::tip Build Streaks
Many achievements reward consistency. Try to do something in Meedi8 every day - even a quick mood check-in counts!
:::

:::tip Use All Features
Mix up your activities. Breathe, journal, track moods, and mediate. You'll unlock badges across all categories.
:::

:::tip Check Silhouette Badges
The hints on silhouette badges tell you what to work toward next. These are your aspirational goals.
:::
