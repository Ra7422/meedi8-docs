## Common Issues

### Can't Log In

1. **Clear browser cache and cookies**
2. **Try incognito/private mode**
3. **Try a different browser** (Chrome recommended)
4. **Check your internet connection**

If still having issues, email us with:
- Which login method you're using (Google/Facebook/Telegram)
- Any error messages you see
- Your browser and device

### Session Not Loading

1. **Refresh the page**
2. **Check your internet connection**
3. **Clear cache and try again**
4. **Try a different browser**

### Other Person Can't Join

1. **Verify they have the correct link**
2. **Ensure they're logged in**
3. **Check if the invitation has expired** (7-day limit)
4. **Resend the invitation from your dashboard**

### Payment Issues

1. **Verify your card details are correct**
2. **Check with your bank** for any blocks
3. **Try a different payment method**
4. **Contact [billing@meedi8.com](mailto:billing@meedi8.com)**

## Bug Reports

Found a bug? Help us fix it!

Email [support@meedi8.com](mailto:support@meedi8.com) with:
- **What happened** (describe the issue)
- **Steps to reproduce** (how can we see the bug?)
- **Expected behavior** (what should have happened?)
- **Screenshots** (if applicable)
- **Browser/Device** information
