# Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your Meedi8 subscription at any time.

## How to Cancel

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Optional: Tell us why (helps us improve)

---

## What Happens When You Cancel

### Immediate Effects
- Cancellation is scheduled for end of billing period
- You keep all features until that date
- No further charges

### At End of Billing Period
- Plan reverts to FREE
- Paid features become unavailable
- Active rooms over FREE limit (1) become read-only
- Your data is **not deleted**

---

## Keeping Your Data

When you cancel:
- All your sessions are preserved
- Reports remain accessible
- Account stays active on FREE plan
- You can resubscribe anytime

---

## Refund Policy

### Within 7 Days of Purchase
If you're not satisfied within 7 days of your first paid subscription, contact [billing@meedi8.com](mailto:billing@meedi8.com) for a full refund.

### After 7 Days
Subscriptions are non-refundable after the 7-day period. You'll retain access until the end of your billing cycle.

### Exceptions
We may offer refunds in exceptional circumstances:
- Technical issues preventing use
- Billing errors
- Other special cases

Contact [billing@meedi8.com](mailto:billing@meedi8.com) to discuss.

---

## Resubscribing

Changed your mind? You can resubscribe anytime:

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Choose a plan
3. Enter payment details
4. Features activate immediately

---

## Deleting Your Account

Cancelling your subscription ≠ deleting your account.

To fully delete your account and all data:

1. Go to Settings → Privacy
2. Click **"Delete Account"**
3. Confirm deletion
4. All data is permanently removed

:::danger Permanent Action
Account deletion is **irreversible**. All sessions, reports, and data will be permanently deleted.
:::
