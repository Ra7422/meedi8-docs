# Managing Your Subscription

Learn how to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your Meedi8 subscription.

## Accessing Subscription Settings

1. Log in to Meedi8
2. Click your profile icon
3. Select **Settings**
4. Click **Subscription**

---

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Upgrade

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click **"Upgrade"** next to your desired plan
3. Enter payment details (if first time)
4. Confirm upgrade

### What Happens When You Upgrade

- New features activate **immediately**
- You're charged the prorated amount
- Billing cycle continues from your upgrade date

---

## Downgrading Your Plan

### How to Downgrade

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Confirm downgrade

### What Happens When You Downgrade

- Current features remain until billing period ends
- New (lower) plan starts at next billing cycle
- Active rooms over new limit become read-only

:::warning Before Downgrading
If you have more active rooms than your new plan allows, you'll need to complete or delete some sessions first.
:::

---

## Viewing Your Billing History

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click **"Billing History"**
3. View past invoices and payments

You can download PDF invoices for your records.

---

## Updating Payment Method

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click **"Payment Method"**
3. Add new card or update existing
4. Save changes
