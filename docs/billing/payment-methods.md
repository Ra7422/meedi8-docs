## Security

All payments are processed securely through **Stripe**, a PCI-compliant payment processor.

- Your card details are **never stored** on our servers
- All transactions use **TLS encryption**
- We support **3D Secure** for additional protection

---

## Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
3. Enter your card details
4. Click **"Save"**

---

## Updating Your Payment Method

1. Go to Settings → Subscription
2. Click **"Payment Method"**
3. Add a new card
4. Set as default
5. Remove old card if desired

---

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Update your payment method in Settings
3. Payment will retry automatically
4. Service continues for grace period

### Grace Period

If payment fails, you have **7 days** to update your payment method before features are limited.
