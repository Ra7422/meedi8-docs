# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Meedi8 ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our service.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Name, email address (from OAuth providers)
- **Session Content:** Messages, uploaded files, voice recordings
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely by Stripe (we don't store card details)
- **Communications:** Support requests, feedback

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, session duration
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Log Data:** IP address, timestamps, error logs

---

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide the Service
- Facilitate mediation sessions
- Generate reports and summaries
- Process AI-powered coaching

### To Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns
- Fix bugs and issues
- Develop new features

### To Communicate
- Send session notifications
- Respond to support requests
- Share important updates

### To Process Payments
- Handle subscriptions
- Send receipts
- Prevent fraud

---

## AI and Your Data

### How AI Uses Your Content
Our AI models (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI) process your messages to:
- Provide coaching and mediation
- Generate summaries and reports
- Transcribe voice messages

### What AI Doesn't Do
- We do **not** use your data to train AI models
- Your conversations are **not** shared with AI providers for their training
- AI processing follows our data processing agreements

---

## Data Sharing

### We Do Not Sell Your Data
We never sell your personal information to third parties.

### Service Providers
We share data with trusted providers who help operate our service:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Stripe | Payments | Payment info |
| AWS | Hosting | Encrypted data |
| Anthropic | AI processing | Session content |
| OpenAI | Transcription | Audio files |
| SendGrid | Email | Email address |

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights or safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse

---

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at rest:** AES-256
- **Access controls:** Role-based permissions
- **Regular audits:** Security assessments

---

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted sessions:** Removed within 30 days
- **Deleted accounts:** All data removed within 30 days
- **Backups:** Purged within 90 days

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Correct:** Update inaccurate information
- **Delete:** Request data deletion
- **Export:** Download your data
- **Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from marketing emails

To exercise these rights, contact [privacy@meedi8.com](mailto:privacy@meedi8.com).

---

## Children's Privacy

Meedi8 is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
