# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2025

This policy explains how Meedi8 uses cookies and similar technologies.

---

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## How We Use Cookies

### Essential Cookies

**Required for the service to function.**

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your login session | Session |
| `csrf_token` | Security protection | Session |
| `auth_token` | Authentication | 30 days |

These cookies **cannot be disabled** without breaking the service.

### Functional Cookies

**Remember your preferences.**

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `theme` | Light/dark mode preference | 1 year |
| `language` | Language preference | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies

**Help us understand how you use Meedi8.**

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Analytics | Usage patterns | 2 years |

We use analytics to:
- Understand popular features
- Identify issues
- Improve the service

**You can opt out of analytics in Settings.**

## Managing Cookies

### In Your Browser

Most browsers let you:
- View cookies
- Delete cookies
- Block cookies

**Instructions:**
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)
