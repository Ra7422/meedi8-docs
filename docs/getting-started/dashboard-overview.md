# Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your home base in Meedi8. Here's what you'll find.

---

## Navigation Panel

The **menu button (☰)** in the top-right corner opens the Navigation Panel - your quickest way to navigate and start new sessions.

### Quick Session Creation

When you tap the menu, you'll see two prominent buttons at the bottom:

| Button | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Solo** | Start a private session with MeediMind AI coach (PRO feature) |
| **Duo** | Create a mediation session with another person |

### Panel Options

The Navigation Panel also provides quick access to:

- **Personal Growth** - Log mood, breathing exercises, gratitude journal, achievements
- **Imports** - Connect Telegram or WhatsApp to import chat history
- **What's Meedi?** - FAQ, About Us, Send Feedback, Help Center
- **Sessions** - View all your sessions
- **Pricing** - Subscription plans
- **Referrals** - Invite friends and earn rewards
- **Profile** - Tap your avatar to access your profile
- **Logout** - Sign out securely

---

## Dashboard Tabs

The Sessions Dashboard has three main tabs:

### Sessions Tab

Your active mediation sessions with:
- **Quick Action Cards** - Fast ways to start sessions
- **Recent Contacts** - Quickly start mediation with past contacts
- **Active Sessions List** - All your ongoing sessions

### Wellness Tab

Track your personal growth journey:
- **Daily Challenges** - Tasks to build healthy habits
- **Mood History** - View your mood patterns over time
- **Gratitude Journal** - Review past gratitude entries
- **Notification Preferences** - Manage alerts

### History Tab

- **Score History Chart** - Track your HealthScore progress over time

---

## Gamification Stats

At the top of your dashboard, you'll always see:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **HealthScore** | Your overall wellness score with tier badge |
| **Streak Counter** | Consecutive days of engagement |

These stats update as you complete sessions, log moods, and engage with wellness activities.

---

## Session Cards

Each session card shows key information at a glance. Tap to expand for more details.

### Collapsed View

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Category Icon** | Visual indicator of session topic |
| **Player Badge** | Shows Solo (1) or Duo (2) session |
| **Title** | Session topic/description |
| **Room Number** | Unique session identifier |
| **Timer** | Remaining time for solo sessions |
| **LED Status** | Red/Orange/Green indicator for duo sessions |

### Expanded View

Tap any card to reveal:
- **Status Badge** - Current phase and description
- **Participants** - Who you're mediating with
- **Start Date** - When the session began
- **Downloads** - Transcript, Report, Files
- **Session Stats** - Voice messages, Telegram imports
- **Action Buttons** - Continue or delete session

### Status Indicators

**For Solo Sessions:**
- **In Session** (purple) - Active coaching with MeediMind
- **Paused** (orange) - Session paused, tap to resume
- **Session Complete** (green) - Finished, view summary

**For Duo Sessions:**
- **In Coaching** (blue) - You're completing your coaching
- **Waiting for Partner** (red LED) - Partner hasn't joined yet
- **Partner in Coaching** (orange LED) - Partner is preparing
- **Ready for Conversation** (green LED) - Both ready to begin
- **In Conversation** (purple) - Mediation in progress

## Quick Actions

### Start New Session

Use the **Navigation Panel** or the **+ New** button in the Active Sessions section:
1. Choose **Solo** (just you with AI) or **Duo** (with someone)
2. Describe your situation
3. Begin coaching

### Continue Session

Tap any session card, then click the action button:
- **Continue Coaching** - Resume your preparation
- **Enter Main Room** - Join the mediation
- **Resume Session** - Continue paused solo session

### Download Files

Expanded cards show available downloads:
- **Transcript** - Full conversation history (TXT)
- **Report** - Professional session analysis (PDF/TXT)
- **Files** - Uploaded attachments

---

## Bulk Actions

### Select Sessions

- Use the checkbox on each card to select
- Click **Select All** to select all sessions
- A floating delete bar appears when sessions are selected

### Delete Sessions

- **Individual** - Expand card and click the trash icon
- **Bulk** - Select multiple sessions and click **Delete Selected**

:::warning Permanent Deletion
Deleting a session permanently removes all messages, recordings, and reports. This cannot be undone.
:::
