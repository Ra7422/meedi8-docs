# Your First Session

Ready to try Meedi8? This guide walks you through your first mediation session.

## Choose Your Session Type

### Solo Session
Work through your thoughts privately with Meedi. Best for:
- Processing emotions before a difficult conversation
- Gaining clarity on your feelings and needs
- Preparing what you want to communicate

### Duo Mediation
Have a guided conversation with another person. Best for:
- Resolving ongoing conflicts
- Having difficult conversations constructively
- Working through misunderstandings

:::tip Start with Solo
We recommend starting with a **Solo session** to get comfortable with the platform and prepare your thoughts.
:::

---

## Starting a Solo Session

### Step 1: Click "New Session"
From your dashboard, click the **New Session** button.

### Step 2: Select "Solo"
Choose the Solo option for a private session.

### Step 3: Describe the Situation
Tell Meedi what's on your mind. You can:
- Type your thoughts
- Record a voice message
- Upload relevant files

**Example prompts:**
- "I had an argument with my partner about household chores"
- "I'm frustrated with a coworker who keeps missing deadlines"
- "I need to have a difficult conversation with my parents"

### Step 4: Engage with Meedi
Meedi will ask questions to help you:
- Identify your feelings
- Understand your underlying needs
- Prepare constructive language

### Step 5: Get Your Summary
When you're ready to finish, Meedi provides:
- A summary of your situation
- Your key feelings and needs
- Suggested talking points

---

## Starting a Duo Mediation

### Step 1: Click "New Session"
From your dashboard, click **New Session**.

### Step 2: Select "Mediation"
Choose the Mediation option for a duo session.

### Step 3: Complete Your Coaching
Before involving the other person, you'll have a private coaching session with Meedi (just like Solo).

### Step 4: Share the Invite Link
When coaching is complete, you'll get a unique link. Share it with the other person via:
- Email
- Text message
- Any messaging app

### Step 5: Wait for Partner
The other person needs to:
1. Click the link
2. Create an account (if needed)
3. Complete their own coaching session

### Step 6: Joint Mediation
Once both are ready, you'll enter the mediation room where:
- Meedi facilitates turn-by-turn conversation
- Each person speaks, then listens
- Meedi helps translate and summarize
- You work toward understanding

## Tips for Your First Session

### Be Honest
The AI works best when you share openly. Everything is confidential.

### Take Your Time
There's no rush. Use the break feature if needed.

### Trust the Process
NVC techniques may feel different at first, but they're proven effective.

### It's Okay to Stop
You can pause or end any session at any time.

---

## What to Expect

**Coaching Phase (15-30 min)**
- Share your perspective
- Identify feelings and needs
- Prepare for dialogue

**Mediation Phase (30-60 min)** *(Duo only)*
- Turn-by-turn conversation
- AI facilitates and summarizes
- Work toward understanding

**Report**
- Summary of key points
- Agreed actions
- Next steps

---

## Common First-Time Questions

### "What if I don't know what to say?"
Meedi will guide you with questions. Just start with what's on your mind.

### "What if I get emotional?"
That's completely normal and okay. Meedi is designed to help you work through emotions constructively.

### "What if the other person gets angry?"
Meedi monitors the conversation and will intervene if things get too heated.
