# Creating Your Account

Get started with Meedi8 in less than 2 minutes.

## Sign Up Options

Meedi8 supports three secure login methods:

| Method | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| **Google** | Quick access if you use Gmail |
| **Facebook** | Easy if you're on Facebook |
| **Telegram** | Great if you want to import chat history |

:::tip Telegram Import
You can connect your Telegram account after signing up with any method to import chat history for context.
:::

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Visit Meedi8

Go to [meedi8.vercel.app](https://meedi8.vercel.app)

### 2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign In"

You'll see the login page with three options.

### 3. Choose Your Login Method

**Google:**
1. Click "Continue with Google"
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant permission

**Facebook:**
1. Click "Continue with Facebook"
2. Log in to Facebook if needed
3. Grant permission

**Telegram:**
1. Click "Continue with Telegram"
2. Enter your phone number
3. Confirm in Telegram app

### 4. Complete Your Profile

After first login, you may be asked to:
- Confirm your display name
- Accept Terms of Service
- Choose notification preferences

### 5. You're In!

You'll be taken to your dashboard where you can start your first session.

## What Happens to My Data?

- We only receive basic profile info (name, email)
- We **never** see your password
- We **never** post on your behalf
- You can revoke access anytime

See our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy) for details.

## Troubleshooting

### Login Button Not Working?
1. Make sure pop-ups are enabled
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear cache and cookies

### "Account Already Exists" Error?
You may have previously signed up with a different method. Try other login options.

### Need Help?
Contact [support@meedi8.com](mailto:support@meedi8.com)
